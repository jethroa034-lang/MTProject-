Products of San Nicolas

Celebrating the timeless artistry and craftsmanship born from the clay of San Nicolas.

Authentic Local Creations

Damili Pottery

The heart of San Nicolas’ identity. Damili, meaning “to mold,” is the town’s age-old pottery craft that turns native red clay into jars, pots, and art pieces reflecting Ilocano tradition.

Clay Cooking Pots (Banga)

Used for generations by Ilocano households, these pots are crafted by San Nicolas potters using local clay — perfect for slow-cooked native dishes.

Red Clay Bricks

Produced from the same earth used in pottery, these bricks are a symbol of durability and craftsmanship, widely used in building ancestral houses and churches.

Decorative Clay Jars

Modern artisans of San Nicolas now create burnay-inspired jars and figurines, merging traditional pottery with contemporary design for tourism and export.

Damili Souvenirs

Miniature jars, figurines, and keychains made from local clay — crafted as cultural tokens that promote San Nicolas’ artistic heritage.
